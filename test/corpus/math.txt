===
Inline math
===

The equation $x^2 + y^2 = z^2$ is famous.

---

(document
  (text)
  (inline_math
    (math_expression))
  (text))

===
Display math block
===

$$
E = mc^2
$$

---

(document
  (text)
  (display_math
    (math_delimiter)
    (math_content)
    (math_delimiter)))

===
Math does not conflict with variables
===

Price is {$amount} dollars.

---

(document
  (text)
  (variable_interpolation
    (variable_path))
  (text))

===
Dollar signs mid-line are not display math
===

The cost is $$50 per unit.

---

(document
  (text)
  (ERROR)
  (inline_math
    (math_expression)
    (MISSING "$")))
